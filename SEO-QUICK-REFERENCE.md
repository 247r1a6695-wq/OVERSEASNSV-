```
TITLE (30-60 chars):

"complete-guide-study-abroad-indian-students"

"Comprehensive guide to studying abroad for Indian students. Learn visa requirements, scholarship options, top universities, and step-by-step application process."

CONTENT STRUCTURE:
├── H1: Title (auto from Blog Title)
├── Introduction (100-200 words)

├── H2: Visa Requirements

│   ├── H3: USA Universities
│   ├── H3: Canada Universities
│   └── H3: UK Universities
```

## 💡 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

```
❌ DON'T: Keyword stuff (using keyword too many times)
✅ DO: Use keyword naturally 2-3 times in content

❌ DON'T: Write only 500 words
✅ DO: Aim for 1500+ words

❌ DON'T: Use long, complex sentences
✅ DO: Keep sentences 15-20 words max

❌ DON'T: Have no internal links
✅ DO: Link to 3-5 other relevant blogs

❌ DON'T: Skip alt text on images
✅ DO: Add descriptive alt text to all images

❌ DON'T: Write only for keywords
✅ DO: Write for real people first, then optimize

❌ DON'T: Publish without checking grammar
✅ DO: Use Grammarly and Hemingway Editor

❌ DON'T: Use generic meta description
✅ DO: Make it compelling, include keyword

❌ DON'T: Skip heading structure
✅ DO: Use H2 and H3 for organization
```
