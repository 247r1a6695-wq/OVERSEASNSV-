How Our Referral Program Works

1

Share Your Referral Code

Get your unique referral code and share it with your friends and connections who are interested in studying abroad.

2

Friend Takes Consultation

Your friend connects with NSV Overseas using your referral code and takes a free counselling session with our expert advisors.

3

Complete Enrollment

Your friend enrolls in any of our programs or takes admission in their chosen university through our guidance.

4

Get Your Rewards

Once the referral is confirmed, earn up to ₹50,000 in rewards. The more referrals, the more you earn!
