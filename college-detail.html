Frequently Asked Questions

What are the application deadlines?

Application deadlines vary by program and intake (Fall, Spring, Winter). Generally, Fall intake applications close by May-June, while Spring intake closes by November-December. We recommend starting your application process early to ensure all documents are submitted on time.

Is financial aid available for international students?

Yes, many universities offer scholarships and financial aid to international students based on academic merit, financial need, or specific criteria. Merit-based scholarships often cover partial to full tuition. We can help you explore available options.

What is the IELTS or TOEFL requirement?

English language requirements depend on your undergraduate degree. If you completed your degree in English, you may be exempt. Otherwise, IELTS (6.5-7.0) or TOEFL (90-100) is typically required. Some universities offer conditional admission with ESL programs.

How do I apply for a student visa?

Once you receive an admission offer, the university provides documentation needed for visa application. Requirements typically include: acceptance letter, proof of financial support, passport, and health checks. We provide comprehensive visa guidance throughout the process.

Can I work while studying?

Most countries allow international students to work part-time (15-20 hours/week) during studies and full-time during semester breaks. This helps offset living expenses. Specific work allowances vary by country and visa conditions.
